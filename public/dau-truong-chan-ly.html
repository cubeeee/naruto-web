"Shinobi Infinity War" is an idle mobile card game with generous rewards. The game recreates classic anime events and characters. Ninja can train their willpower and grow on their own, or team up with other players in PVP battles. Experience thrilling ninja duels and build your very own Hidden Leaf Village! [Faithful Anime Recreation] Explore the authentic scenery of the Hidden Leaf Village. Relive iconic moments from the Chunin Exams, the Great Three-Way Ninja War, the chase for Sasuke, the Battle at the Valley of the End... and reignite your passion for battle! [Collect Ninja, Fight Your Way] Create your own legendary ninja. From the Twelve Ninja of the Hidden Leaf, the Jounin, the Legendary Three Ninja, the ninja of the Land of Sand, and hundreds of other renowned ninja, your adventure will never be a lonely one! [Survival Challenge, Conquer the Ninja World] Pick the strongest ninja, build the strongest lineup, make friends, challenge top players, and fight side by side to earn generous rewards as you clear the levels! Download and play Shinobi Infinity War today!
